Common Questions

FAQs

Do I need to be a fully qualified ADI? ▼
Not necessarily. We welcome fully qualified ADIs and PDIs who are currently in training. If you're not yet qualified but interested, get in touch and we can point you in the right direction.
Can I work anywhere in the UK? ▼
Yes — no territory restrictions. You operate in your local area under the Drivin Guru brand. We're actively recruiting across England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland.
Do I need my own car? ▼
Yes, you'll need your own dual-controlled vehicle. If you don't currently have one, we can point you towards leasing options that work well for instructors.
How does the payment split work? ▼
Fair and transparent — you keep the majority of your lesson fees. Full details are discussed during your onboarding conversation. No nasty surprises.
How quickly will I get students? ▼
We actively invest in Google Ads and social media to generate enquiries in your area. Most instructors start receiving leads within the first few weeks of being set up.
Is this employed or self-employed? ▼
Self-employed. You operate under the Drivin Guru brand with full flexibility over your hours and earnings — with the benefit of our brand and marketing support behind you.
